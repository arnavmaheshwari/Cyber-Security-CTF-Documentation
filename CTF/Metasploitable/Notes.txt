o http-post-form type request
o /dvwa/login.php:username=^USER^&password=^PASS^&Login=Login:Login failed : site address, after the IP address. WE write ^USER^ and ^PASS^ to allow hydra to fill in those spaces. The Login= part tells that the password is incorrect on the specified error message.
